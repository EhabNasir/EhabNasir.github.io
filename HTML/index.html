About Me

I’m Ehab, a 21-year-old games programming student and developer from the UK. I specialize in Unity (C#) and Unreal Engine (C++), where I enjoy programming AI, gameplay and physics systems.

In my early days I created small board games and experimented with Scratch and Gamemaker, which grew into a passion for coding and engine-based development. Since then, I’ve actively participated in game jams and personal projects, constantly challenging myself to expand my technical and creative skills.

I thrive in collaborative environments and enjoy both developing gameplay systems and working with others to bring ideas to life. My goal is to contribute to a studio where I can continue pushing boundaries while delivering engaging player experiences.
